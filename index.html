🇮🇳 इस्तेमाल करने की गाइड

Yeh tool aapko kisi bhi business ko khareedne (acquire karne) se pehle uska poora health-check karne mein madad karta hai.

Step 1: Business Details Bharein

Business ka naam, industry, employees aur operation ke saal select karein.

Step 2: 20 Due-Diligence Sawaalon Ke Jawab Dein

Financial Health, Market Position, Operational Risk, Legal Compliance aur Growth Potential — in 5 categories ke sawaal honestly answer karein. Jitna accurate jawab, utna sahi score.

Step 3: Valuation Numbers Daalein

Revenue, Profit/EBITDA, Assets, Liabilities aur (agar pata ho) seller ki asking price daalein.

Step 4: Analyze Button Dabayein

Aapko turant Acquisition Score (0-100), category breakdown, aur estimated valuation range mil jayega — bilkul FREE.

Step 5: Full Report Unlock Karein (Optional)

₹149 mein lifetime unlock karke AI se detailed Hinglish report paayein — Strengths, Red Flags, Negotiation Points aur Final Recommendation (Buy / Negotiate / Avoid).

Note

Yeh tool ek starting point hai. Bade acquisitions ke liye hamesha ek CA ya lawyer se legal & financial due diligence zaroor karayein.
